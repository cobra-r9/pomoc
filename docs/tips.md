Here comes the interesting section. There is a lot more thing we can do with a simple pomodoro, that is built around the Unix philosophy.

## Based on Window Managers

People who use bspwm, who are keyboard centric, need a fast way to toggle the start/pause/end in pomoc, you can add the following keybindings (provided they are not conflicting with your other keybindings)

```txt
Pause
    pomoc toggle

shift + Pause
    pomoc end
```

Similarly, other users can also try it, based on their window managers.
